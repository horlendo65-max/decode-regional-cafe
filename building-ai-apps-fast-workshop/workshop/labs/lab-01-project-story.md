# Lab 01 — Project Story and Architecture

## Goal

Understand what the project is, why it uses agents/skills/workflows and what a successful demo looks like.

## Activity

1. Read `docs/project-overview.md`.
2. Open `docs/architecture.md`.
3. Identify the agent, skills and workflow in the repo.

## Checkpoint

You can explain the app in one sentence:

> This app analyses M-PESA statement text and gives budgeting recommendations using an Expense Agent, reusable skills and a Gemini-powered workflow.

# Lab 06 — Demo and Extensions

## Goal

Prepare a short demo and choose one next extension.

## Demo structure

1. State the problem.
2. Paste M-PESA statement text.
3. Show the AI result.
4. Explain the agent/skill/workflow architecture.
5. Share your live URL.

## Extension options

- Daraja Sandbox STK Push
- Health decision tool
- Agriculture advisor
- PDF/CSV statement upload
- Multi-domain agent switcher

## Checkpoint

You can explain what you built, how it works and what you would improve next.
